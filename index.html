Prayers of the Prophet (ﷺ)
It has been narrated that the Messenger of Allah (ﷺ) said: A PRAYER RECOMMENDED TO BE READ 100 TIMES EVERY DAY
لاَ إِلَهَ إِلاَّ اللَّهُ وَحْدَهُ لاَ شَرِيكَ لَهُ، لَهُ الْمُلْكُ وَلَهُ الْحَمْدُ وَهُوَ عَلَى كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيرٌ
"There is no god but Allah, alone, without any partner. To Him belongs sovereignty, and to Him belongs all praise, and He is over all things competent."
If one says these one hundred times a day, he will be given the reward of freeing ten slaves...
(Bukhari, Bad'u'l-Halk, 11)
Hasan Basri (RA) used to make the following supplication:
اللَّهُمَّ أَغْنِنِي بِالْفَقْرِ إِلَيْكَ وَلاَ تُفْقِرْنِي بِالاِسْتِغْنَاءِ عَنْكَ
"O Allah! Enrich me by making me dependent on You, and do not make me poor by making me independent of You!"
On one occasion, the Messenger of Allah (ﷺ) said: "If a Muslim upon whom a calamity has befallen says..."
إِنَّا لِلَّهِ وَإِنَّا إِلَيْهِ رَاجِعُونَ. اللَّهُمَّ أَجِرْنِي فِي مُصِيبَتِي وَأَخْلِفْ لِي خَيْرًا مِنْهَا
"Indeed, to Allah we belong, and to Him we shall return. O Allah, reward me in my calamity and replace it with something better for me!"
(Muslim, Janaiz, 3)
Guidance Prayer (Istikhara):
اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْتَخِيرُكَ بِعِلْمِكَ وَأَسْتَقْدِرُكَ بِقُدْرَتِكَ...
'O Allah, I seek Your counsel by Your knowledge...'
(Bukhari)
When leaving the house:
بِسْمِ اللَّهِ تَوَكَّلْتُ عَلَى اللَّهِ اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ أَنْ أَضِلَّ أَوْ أُضَلَّ...
"In the Name of Allah, I have put my trust in Allah..."
(Abu Dawud)
Prayer for Need:
لاَ إِلَهَ إِلاَّ اللَّهُ الْحَلِيمُ الْكَرِيمُ. سُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ رَبِّ الْعَرْشِ الْعَظِيمِ...
"There is no god but Allah, the Forbearing, the Generous..."
(Tirmidhi)
Protection from Evils:
أَعُوذُ بِكَلِمَاتِ اللَّهِ التَّامَّاتِ مِنْ شَرِّ مَا خَلَقَ
'I seek refuge in the perfect words of Allah from the evil of what He has created.'
(Muslim)
Before Sleeping:
اللَّهُمَّ أَسْلَمْتُ نَفْسِي إِلَيْكَ...
"O Allah! I have surrendered myself to You..."
(Bukhari)
During Salah Before Salam:
اللَّهُمَّ اغْفِرْ لِي مَا قَدَّمْتُ وَمَا أَخَّرْتُ...
"Ya Allah, forgive me for the sins I have committed..."
(Muslim)
Refuge from Hypocrisy:
اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ الشِّقَاقِ وَالنِّفَاقِ وَسُوءِ الأَخْلاقِ
"O Allah! I seek refuge in You from dissension, from hypocrisy, and from bad manners."
(Abu Dawud)
Witr Prayer:
اللَّهُمَّ اهْدِنِي فِيمَنْ هَدَيْتَ...
"O Allah, include me among those whom You have guided..."
(Tirmidhi)
Against Bad Omens:
اللَّهُمَّ لاَ يَأْتِي بِالْحَسَنَاتِ إِلاَّ أَنْتَ...
'O Allah! Only You bring good deeds...'
(Abu Dawud)
To Unite Hearts:
اللَّهُمَّ أَلِّفْ بَيْنَ قُلُوبِنَا...
"O Allah! Unite our hearts..."
(Abu Dawud)
Protection from Calamities:
اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنَ التَّرَدِّي...
"O Allah! I seek refuge in You from falling from a height..."
(Abu Dawud)
For Faith:
اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ إِيمَانًا لاَ يَرْتَدُّ...
"O Allah! I ask You for faith that does not falter..."
(Ahmed)
Truthful Entry:
رَبِّ أَدْخِلْنِي مُدْخَلَ صِدْقٍ...
"...My Lord! Grant me to enter with a truthful entry..."
(Quran)
To Thank Someone:
جَزَاكَ اللَّهُ خَيْرًا
"May Allah reward you with good."
(Tirmidhi)
PROPHET ADAM'S (AS) SUPPLICATION

Blessing in Sustenance:
اللَّهُمَّ بَارِكْ لَنَا فِي مِدِّنَا وَصَاعِنَا
"O Allah! Bless our products that we measure and weigh."
(Bukhari)
Our Prophet (ﷺ), when our mother Aisha (may Allah be pleased with her) was angry, advised her to make the following dua:
اللَّهُمَّ اغْفِرْ لِي ذَنْبِي وَأَذْهِبْ غَيْظَ قَلْبِي وَأَجِرْنِي مِنْ مُضِلاَّتِ الْفِتَنِ
"O Allah! Forgive my sin, remove the anger from my heart, and protect me from misleading trials."
(Tabarani)
Refuge from 4 Things:
أَعُوذُ بِاللَّهِ مِنْ جَهْدِ الْبَلاءِ...
"I seek refuge in Allah from severe affliction..."
(Bukhari)

Prayers after Salah
اللَّه اللَّه * اللَّهُ رَبِّي لَا أُشْرِكُ بِهِ شَيْئًا * اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ بِاسْمِكَ الْعَظِيمِ وَرِضْوَانِكَ الْأَكْبَرِ * يَا ذَا الْجَلَالِ وَالْإكْرَامِ * أَنْ تَفْعَلَ لِي كَذَا
“Al-hamdu li’llahi Rabbil-alamin. As-salatu was-salamu ala Rasulina Muhammadin wa Alihi wa Sahbihi ajmain.
1.
Oh my Rabb! Accept the ritual prayer I’ve performed. Make my end and fate good. Make me say kalimat at-tawhid at my last breath. Forgive and hide the sins of my dead relatives. Allahum-maghfir warham wa anta khayrur-rahimin. Tawaffani Musliman wa al-hiqni bis-salihin. Allahum-maghfirli wa li-walidayya wa li-ustadhiyya wa lil-Muminina wal-Muminat yawma yakumul-hisab.
2.
Oh my Rabb! Protect me from the evil of Satan, the evil of enemies and the evil of my nafs al-ammara! Grant goodness, halal and beneficial sustenance to our home! Grant peace over all Muslims! Overwhelm and ruin the enemies of Muslims! With Your Divine Help, help the Muslims engaged in jihad against unbelievers!
3.
Allahumma innaka afuwwun karimun tuhibbul-afwa fafu anni. Oh my Rabb! Grant recovery to the ill among us, grant relief to those in difficulty among us! Allahumma inni asalukas-sihhata wal-afiyata wal-amanata wa husn al-khulqi war-ridaa bil-qadari bi-rahmatika ya arhamar-rahimin.
4.
Oh my Rabb! Grant a beneficial life, good morals, good sense, good health, afiya (not sinning) and istiqama (following the path Allahu Taala likes) to my parents, children, relatives, friends and to all my brothers-in-Islam.”
5.
Prayer of SalahOh my Allah! I do regret and I am sorry for all wrong, faulty beliefs I might have as a result of getting deceived by enemies of Islam or by heretical people, and for all the heretical or sinful things I might have said, listened to, seen or committed since the beginning of my puberty until this moment; and I am strongly resolute and determined to not believe or act in that wrongful manner. I believe in the first prophet Adam (alayhis-salam) and the last one, our beloved prophet Muhammad (alayhis-salam), and all the prophets who lived between them. All of them were righteous and faithful. What they revealed were true. Amantu billah wa bi-ma jaa min indillah, ala muradillah, wa amantu bi-Rasulillah wa bi-ma jaa min indi Rasulillah ala muradi Rasulillah, amantu billahi wa Malaikatihi wa kutubihi wa Rusulihi wal-yawmil-akhiri wa bil-qadari khayrihi wa sharrihi min-Allahi taala wal-bathu badal-mawti haqqun ashhadu an la ilaha illa’llah wa ashhadu anna Muhammadan abduhu wa rasuluh.

Hizb al-Kifayah (The Litany of Sufficiency)
❁ ❁ ❁
بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَنِ الرَّحِيمِ
Allah - beginning with the name of - the Most Gracious, the Most Merciful
﴿هُوَ اللَّهُ الَّذِي لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا هُوَ عَالِمُ الْغَيْبِ وَالشَّهَادَةِ هُوَ الرَّحْمَنُ الرَّحِيمُ ۞ هُوَ اللَّهُ الَّذِي لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا هُوَ الْمَلِكُ الْقُدُّوسُ السَّلَامُ الْمُؤْمِنُ الْمُهَيْمِنُ الْعَزِيزُ الْجَبَّارُ الْمُتَكَبِّرُ ۞ سُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ عَمَّا يُشْرِكُونَ ۞ هُوَ اللَّهُ الْخَالِقُ الْبَارِئُ الْمُصَوِّرُ لَهُ الْأَسْمَاءُ الْحُسْنَىٰ ۞ يُسَبِّحُ لَهُ مَا فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ وَهُوَ الْعَزِيزُ الْحَكِيمُ﴾
He is Allah, other than whom there is no deity, Knower of the unseen and the witnessed. He is the Entirely Merciful, the Especially Merciful. He is Allah, other than whom there is no deity, the Sovereign, the Pure, the Perfection, the Bestower of Faith, the Overseer, the Exalted in Might, the Compeller, the Superior. Exalted is Allah above whatever they associate with Him. He is Allah, the Creator, the Inventor, the Fashioner; to Him belong the best names. Whatever is in the heavens and earth is exalting Him. And He is the Exalted in Might, the Wise. (59:22-24)
اللَّهُ لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا هُوَ ۞ ﴿وَعَلَى اللَّهِ فَلْيَتَوَكَّلِ الْمُؤْمِنُونَ﴾ ۞ ﴿رَبُّ الْمَشْرِقِ وَالْمَغْرِبِ لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا هُوَ فَاتَّخِذْهُ وَكِيلًا﴾
Allah - there is no deity except Him. And upon Allah let the believers rely. [He is] the Lord of the East and the West; there is no deity except Him, so take Him as Disposer of all affairs.
اللَّهُمَّ أَنْتَ رَبِّي لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا أَنْتَ ۞ عَلَيْكَ تَوَكَّلْتُ وَأَنْتَ رَبُّ الْعَرْشِ الْعَظِيمِ ۞ مَا شَاءَ اللَّهُ كَانَ وَمَا لَمْ يَشَأْ لَمْ يَكُنْ ۞ وَلَا حَوْلَ وَلَا قُوَّةَ إِلَّا بِاللَّهِ الْعَلِيِّ الْعَظِيمِ
O Allah, You are my Lord, there is no god but You. In You I trust and You are the Lord of the Mighty Throne. What Allah wills happens, and what He does not will does not happen. And there is no power nor strength except with Allah, the Most High, the Magnificent.
أَعْلَمُ أَنَّ اللَّهَ عَلَىٰ كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيرٌ ۞ وَأَنَّ اللَّهَ قَدْ أَحَاطَ بِكُلِّ شَيْءٍ عِلْمًا ۞ وَأَنَّ اللَّهَ يَبْعَثُ مَنْ فِي الْقُبُورِ
I know that Allah has power over all things, and that Allah comprehends all things in His knowledge, and that Allah will resurrect those in the graves.
❁ ❁ ❁
اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَعُوذُ بِكَ مِنْ شَرِّ نَفْسِي وَمِنْ شَرِّ الشَّيْطَانِ الرَّجِيمِ ۞ وَمِنْ شَرِّ كُلِّ دَابَّةٍ أَنْتَ آخِذٌ بِنَاصِيَتِهَا ۞ ﴿إِنَّ رَبِّي عَلَىٰ صِرَاطٍ مُّسْتَقِيمٍ﴾ ۞ ﴿فَإِن تَوَلَّوْا فَقُلْ حَسْبِيَ اللَّهُ لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا هُوَ عَلَيْهِ تَوَكَّلْتُ وَهُوَ رَبُّ الْعَرْشِ الْعَظِيمِ﴾
O Allah, I seek refuge in You from the evil of my self, and from the evil of the accursed Satan, and from the evil of every moving creature whose forelock You hold. Indeed, my Lord is on a path that is straight. But if they turn away, say, "Sufficient for me is Allah; there is no deity except Him. On Him I have relied, and He is the Lord of the Great Throne."
بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَنِ الرَّحِيمِ ۞ ﴿فَاللَّهُ خَيْرٌ حَافِظًا وَهُوَ أَرْحَمُ الرَّاحِمِينَ﴾
Allah - beginning with the name of - the Most Gracious, the Most Merciful. "But Allah is the best guardian, and He is the most merciful of the merciful."
آمَنْتُ بِاللَّهِ ۞ وَدَخَلْتُ فِي كَنَفِ اللَّهِ ۞ وَتَحَصَّنْتُ بِكِتَابِ اللَّهِ وَآيَاتِ اللَّهِ ۞ وَاسْتَجَرْتُ بِرَسُولِ اللَّهِ سَيِّدِنَا مُحَمَّدِ بْنِ عَبْدِ اللَّهِ صَلَّى اللَّهُ عَلَيْهِ وَآلِهِ وَسَلَّمَ
I believe in Allah, and I enter into the protection of Allah, and I fortify myself with the Book of Allah and the Verses of Allah, and I seek refuge with the Messenger of Allah, our Master Muhammad bin Abdullah (ﷺ).
اللَّهُ أَكْبَرُ ۞ اللَّهُ أَكْبَرُ مِمَّا أَخَافُ وَأَحْذَرُ ۞ أَعُوذُ بِكَلِمَاتِ اللَّهِ التَّامَّاتِ مِنْ شَرِّ مَا خَلَقَ ۞ بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الَّذِي لَا يَضُرُّ مَعَ اسْمِهِ شَيْءٌ فِي الْأَرْضِ وَلَا فِي السَّمَاءِ وَهُوَ السَّمِيعُ الْعَلِيمُ ۞ حَسْبِيَ اللَّهُ وَنِعْمَ الْوَكِيلُ ۞ وَلَا حَوْلَ وَلَا قُوَّةَ إِلَّا بِاللَّهِ الْعَلِيِّ الْعَظِيمِ
Allah is Greatest. Allah is Greater than what I fear and beware of. I seek refuge in the perfect words of Allah from the evil of what He created. In the Name of Allah, with Whose Name nothing can harm on earth or in the heaven, and He is the All-Hearing, the All-Knowing. Sufficient for me is Allah, and He is the best Disposer of affairs. And there is no power nor strength except with Allah, the Most High, the Magnificent.
بِسْمِ اللَّهِ عَلَى نَفْسِي وَدِينِي وَأَهْلِي وَمَالِي وَعِيَالِي وَأَصْحَابِي ۞ وَعَلَى كُلِّ شَيْءٍ أَعْطَانِيهِ رَبِّي ۞ اللَّهُ الْحَافِظُ الْكَافِي
In the Name of Allah upon my self, my religion, my family, my wealth, my dependents, and my companions. And upon everything my Lord has given me. Allah is the Protector, the Sufficient.
حِزْبُ التَّوَسُّلِ
Hizb at-Tawassul (The Litany of Intercession)
اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَتَوَسَّلُ بِكَ إِلَيْكَ ۞ اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أُقْسِمُ بِكَ عَلَيْكَ ۞ اللَّهُمَّ كَمَا كُنْتَ دَلِيلِي عَلَيْكَ فَكُنْ شَفِيعِي إِلَيْكَ ۞ اللَّهُمَّ إِنَّ حَسَنَاتِي مِنْ عَطَائِكَ ۞ وَسَيِّئَاتِي مِنْ قَضَائِكَ ۞ فَجِدِ اللَّهُمَّ بِمَا أَعْطَيْتَ عَلَى بِمَا بِهِ قَضَيْتَ ۞ حَتَّى تَمْحُوَ ذَلِكَ بِذَلِكَ
O Allah, I seek access to You through You. O Allah, I swear by You upon You. O Allah, just as You were my Guide to You, be my Intercessor to You. O Allah, my good deeds are from Your gift, and my bad deeds are from Your decree. So grant, O Allah, by what You have given, over what You have decreed, until You erase this with that.
يَا مَنْ أَعْطَيْتَ نُوحًا فِي قَوْمِهِ ۞ يَا مَنْ نَصَرَ إِبْرَاهِيمَ عَلَى أَعْدَائِهِ ۞ يَا مَنْ رَدَّ يُوسُفَ عَلَى يَعْقُوبَ ۞ يَا مَنْ كَشَفَ ضُرَّ أَيُّوبَ ۞ يَا مَنْ أَجَابَ دَعْوَةَ زَكَرِيَا ۞ يَا مَنْ قَبِلَ تَسْبِيحَ يُونُسَ بْنَ مَتَّى
O You who gave Noah among his people. O You who helped Abraham against his enemies. O You who returned Joseph to Jacob. O You who removed the affliction of Job. O You who answered the prayer of Zechariah. O You who accepted the praise of Jonah son of Matta.
نَسْأَلُكَ بِأَسْرَارِ هَذِهِ الدَّعَوَاتِ ۞ أَنْ تَتَقَبَّلَ مَا بِهِ دَعَوْنَاكَ ۞ وَأَنْ تُعْطِيَنَا مَا سَأَلْنَاكَ ۞ أَنْجِزْ لَنَا وَعْدَكَ الَّذِي وَعَدْتَهُ لِعِبَادِكَ الْمُؤْمِنِينَ
We ask You by the secrets of these supplications, that You accept what we have prayed to You for, and that You give us what we have asked You. Fulfill for us Your promise which You promised to Your believing servants.
لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا أَنْتَ سُبْحَانَكَ إِنِّي كُنْتُ مِنَ الظَّالِمِينَ ۞ انْقَطَعَتْ آمَالُنَا وَعِزَّتِكَ إِلَّا مِنْكَ ۞ وَخَابَ رَجَاؤُنَا وَحَقِّكَ إِلَّا فِيكَ
There is no god but You; exalted are You. Indeed, I have been of the wrongdoers. Our hopes have been cut off, by Your Glory, except from You. And our hopes have been disappointed, by Your Truth, except in You.
إِنْ أَبْطَأَتْ غَارَةُ الْأَرْحَامِ وَابْتَعَدَتْ فَأَقْرَبُ الشَيْءِ مِنَّا غَارَةُ اللَّهِ ۞ يَا غَارَةَ اللَّهِ جِدِّي السَّيْرَ مُسْرِعَةً فِي حَلِّ عُقْدَتِنَا يَا غَارَةَ اللَّهِ
If the help of relatives is slow and distant, then the nearest thing to us is the Aid of Allah. O Aid of Allah! Come quickly to untie our knots, O Aid of Allah!
عَدَتِ الْعَادُونَ وَجَارُوا ۞ وَرَجَوْنَا اللَّهَ مُجِيرًا ۞ وَكَفَى بِاللَّهِ وَلِيًّا ۞ وَكَفَى بِاللَّهِ نَصِيرًا
The aggressors have attacked and oppressed, and we hope in Allah as a Protector. And sufficient is Allah as a Guardian, and sufficient is Allah as a Helper.
حَسْبُنَا اللَّهُ وَنِعْمَ الْوَكِيلُ ۞ وَلَا حَوْلَ وَلَا قُوَّةَ إِلَّا بِاللَّهِ الْعَلِيِّ الْعَظِيمِ ۞ اسْتَجِبْ لَنَا ۞ آمِينَ
Sufficient for us is Allah, and He is the best Disposer of affairs. And there is no power nor strength except with Allah, the Most High, the Magnificent. Answer us. Ameen.
فَقُطِعَ دَابِرُ الْقَوْمِ الَّذِينَ ظَلَمُوا ۞ وَالْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ الْعَالَمِينَ ۞ حَسْبُنَا اللَّهُ وَنِعْمَ الْوَكِيلُ (450)
So the roots of the people who did wrong were cut off. And praise to Allah, Lord of the worlds. Sufficient for us is Allah, and He is the best Disposer of affairs.
وَعَلَى كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيرٌ ۞ عَبْدًا لَا يَمْلِكُ دُنْيَا وَلَا أُخْرَى ۞ إِنَّكَ عَلَى كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيرٌ ۞ وَصَلَّى اللَّهُ عَلَى سَيِّدِنَا مُحَمَّدٍ وَعَلَى آلِهِ وَصَحْبِهِ وَسَلَّمَ
And He has power over all things. A servant who possesses neither this world nor the Hereafter. Indeed, You have power over all things. And may Allah bless our Master Muhammad (ﷺ) and his family and companions and grant them peace.

Protects one from all calamities
بِسْمِ ٱللَّهِ ٱلرَّحْمَٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ
قُلْ هُوَ ٱللَّهُ أَحَدٌ ﴿١﴾ ٱللَّهُ ٱلصَّمَدُ ﴿٢﴾ لَمْ يَلِدْ وَلَمْ يُولَدْ ﴿٣﴾ وَلَمْ يَكُن لَّهُۥ كُفُوًا أَحَدٌۢ ﴿٤﴾
1. Say you, He is Allah, the One. 2. Allah the Independent, Care free. 3. He begot none nor was He begotten. 4. And nor anyone is equal to Him.
بِسْمِ ٱللَّهِ ٱلرَّحْمَٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ
قُلْ أَعُوذُ بِرَبِّ ٱلْفَلَقِ ﴿١﴾ مِن شَرِّ مَا خَلَقَ ﴿٢﴾ وَمِن شَرِّ غَاسِقٍ إِذَا وَقَبَ ﴿٣﴾ وَمِن شَرِّ ٱلنَّفَّٰثَٰتِ فِي ٱلْعُقَدِ ﴿٤﴾ وَمِن شَرِّ حَاسِدٍ إِذَا حَسَدَ ﴿٥﴾
1. Say you, I take refuge with the Lord of Daybreak. 2. From the evil of all creatures; 3. And from the evil of the darkening one when it sets. 4. And from the evil of those women who blow in the knots. 5. And from the evil of the envier when he envies me.
بِسْمِ ٱللَّهِ ٱلرَّحْمَٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ
قُلْ أَعُوذُ بِرَبِّ ٱلنَّاسِ ﴿١﴾ مَلِكِ ٱلنَّاسِ ﴿٢﴾ إِلَٰهِ ٱلنَّاسِ ﴿٣﴾ مِن شَرِّ ٱلْوَسْوَاسِ ٱلْخَنَّاسِ ﴿٤﴾ ٱلَّذِى يُوَسْوِسُ فِى صُدُورِ ٱلنَّاسِ ﴿٥﴾ مِنَ ٱلْجِنَّةِ وَٱلنَّاسِ ﴿٦﴾
1. Say you, I came in the refuge of the Lord of all mankind; 2. King of all, 3. God of all, 4. From the evil of him who whispers evil designs in the heart and slinks away. 5. Those who whisper in the hearts of mankind. 6. Jinn and men.
Protects from ills during day & night
فَسُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ حِينَ تُمْسُونَ وَحِينَ تُصْبِحُونَ وَلَهُ الْحَمْدُ فِي السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ وَعَشِيًّا وَحِينَ تُظْهِرُونَ. يُخْرِجُ الْحَيَّ مِنَ الْمَيِّتِ وَيُخْرِجُ الْمَيِّتَ مِنَ الْحَيِّ وَيُحْيِي الْأَرْضَ بَعْدَ مَوْتِهَا وَكَذَلِكَ تُخْرَجُونَ
Therefore, glorify Allah when you enter the evening and when you enter the morning. And His is praise in the heavens and in the earth, and at little before the setting of sun and when you enter the noon. He brings forth the living from the dead and brings forth the dead from the living, and gives life to the earth after its death. And in like manner you shall be brought forth.
Protection from Shaytaan and evil Jinn
أَفَحَسِبْتُمْ أَنَّمَا خَلَقْنَاكُمْ عَبَثًا وَأَنَّكُمْ إِلَيْنَا لَا تُرْجَعُونَ. فَتَعَالَى اللَّهُ الْمَلِكُ الْحَقُّ لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا هُوَ رَبُّ الْعَرْشِ الْكَرِيمِ. وَمَنْ يَدْعُ مَعَ اللَّهِ إِلَهًا آَخَرَ لَا بُرْهَانَ لَهُ بِهِ فَإِنَّمَا حِسَابُهُ عِنْدَ رَبِّهِ إِنَّهُ لَا يُفْلِحُ الْكَافِرُونَ. وَقُلْ رَبِّ اغْفِرْ وَارْحَمْ وَأَنْتَ خَيْرُ الرَّاحِمِينَ
Do you then think that We made you in vain and you are not to return to Us? Then exalted high is Allah, the True King; there is no god except He the Lord of Honoured Throne. And whosoever worships any other god beside Allah, for which he has no proof, then his account is with his Lord. Undoubtedly, the infidels have no rescue. And you submit, 'O my Lord forgive and have mercy and You are the best of merciful.
Protection from snakes and reptiles
أَعُوذُ بِكَلِمَاتِ اللهِ التَّامَّاتِ مِنْ شَرِّ مَا خَلَقَ
I seek refuge in the perfect (complete) words of Allah from the evil in what He created. (3 Times)
Protection against any poison
بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الَّذِي لَا يَضُرُّ مَعَ اسْمِهِ شَيْءٌ فِي الْأَرْضِ وَلَا فِي السَّمَاءِ وَهُوَ السَّمِيعُ الْعَلِيمُ
In the name of Allah by whose Name nothing harms on earth or in the heavens and He is All-Hearing All-Knowing. (3 Times)
To please Allah on Day of Judgement
رَضِيتُ بِاللهِ رَبًّا وَبِالْإِسْلَامِ دِينًا وَبِمُحَمَّدٍ صَلَّى اللهُ عَلَيْهِ وَسَلَّمَ نَبِيًّا وَرَسُولًا
I am content with God as the Lord and with Islam as the religion, and with Muhammad as the Prophet and the Messenger. (3 Times)
Protection from all ills and torments
حَسْبِيَ اللَّهُ لاَ إِلَهَ إِلاَّ هُوَ عَلَيْهِ تَوَكَّلْتُ وَهُوَ رَبُّ الْعَرْشِ الْعَظِيمِ
God suffices me. There is no God but Him; on Him do I rely, and He is the magnificent Lord of the throne. (3 Times)
Suffice for all needs
سُبْحَانَ اللهِ وَبِحَمْدِهِ وَلاَ حَوْلَ وَلاَ قُوَّةَ إِلاَّ بِاللهِ
مَا شَاءَ اللهُ كَانَ وَمَا لَمْ يَشَأْلَمْ يَكُنْ
أَعْلَمُ أَنَّ اللهَ عَلَى كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيرٌ
وَأَنَّ اللهَ قَدْ أَحَاطَ بِكُلِّ شَيْءٍ عِلْمًا
Glory be to Allah and all praise. There is no might and no power except from Allah. Whatever He wished has happened, and whatever He has not wished has not happened. I acknowledge and am certain that Allah has power over everything and Allah's eternal knowledge is encompassing everything.
